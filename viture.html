Play Anywhere
VITURE One supports remote play with your PlayStation, Xbox, or PC anywhere on a portable 120-inch virtual screen. It also supports Switch dock mode and co-op play on the go.
STREAM SHOWS & MOVIES
Relax in your own personal theater anywhere, anytime: see all your favorite video content on the virtual big-screen with native support for AppleTV, Hulu, HBOMax and other streaming services.
SWITCH ON-THE-GO
Connect your Nintendo Switch to VITURE One with our Mobile Dock accessory and squeeze in a couple games of Smash on your commute from work or school - you can even sync up with another VITURE One wearer for on-the-go co-op play.
IMMERSIVE STEAM DECK EXPERIENCE
The power of PC gaming on a portable 120" display - connect your Steam Deck to VITURE One and give your game library the fully immersive experience it deserves.
